UT Queer & Trans Report

Our team is led by LGBTQIA+ students and works for LGBTQIA+ students. This report is an initiative that aims to gauge the perception of campus resources and student experiences by the LGBTQIA+ community. We will utilize the findings to propel the necessary change at UT Austin.

Focus Groups

We plan to conduct a variety of focus groups to learn more about the different experiences of LGBTQIA+ members in different communities. Currently, we plan to compensate focus group participants approximately $20. If this is something you think you would be interested in, keep an eye out for more information on this page and on our social media!
